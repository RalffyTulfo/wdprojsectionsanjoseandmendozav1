When the stars aligned...

Born Peter Gene Hernandez Jr., Bruno Mars is a Filipino-American dancer, singer, songwriter, record producer, and actor. After co-writing numerous popular songs, he signed with Atlantic Records in 2002 and became well-known as a solo singer. His album, Doo-Wops [&] Hooligans, was published in 2010 and peaked at number three on the Billboard 200 chart. Among his singles, "Just the Way You Are," "Nothin' on You," "Grenade," "Locked Out of Heaven," "When I Was Your Man," "The Lazy Song," "It Will Rain," "24K Magic," and "Finesse," all made it to the top five of the US Billboard Hot 100. Mars received seven Grammy nominations and won Best Male Pop Vocal Performance for "Just the Way You Are." In 2016, he released the single "24K Magic," which reached the top five of the Billboard Hot 100, and in 2021, he collaborated with recording artist Anderson Paak on the song "Leave the Door Open," becoming his eighth and Paak's first number-one on the Billboard Hot 100.
